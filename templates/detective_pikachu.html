Movie Summary

Pokémon Detective Pikachu is a 2019 urban fantasy mystery film. The story follows Tim Goodman, a former Pokémon Trainer who partners with Detective Pikachu to uncover the mysterious disappearance of his father, Harry Goodman. Set in the vibrant Ryme City, where humans and Pokémon coexist, the movie brings many beloved Pokémon to life with stunning visual effects.

From the witty and charming Pikachu to the fierce Charizard, the movie features an array of Pokémon that adds excitement and depth to the storyline. Whether you're a fan of the original games or new to the world of Pokémon, Detective Pikachu is a delightful experience for all ages.

Detective Pikachu is not just a movie; it's a celebration of the Pokémon universe. Whether it's the interactions between humans and Pokémon, the vivid portrayal of Ryme City, or the mystery that unfolds, the film brings to life a unique blend of action, humor, and emotional depth.
